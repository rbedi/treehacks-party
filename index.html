FAQ

Who is coming?

TreeHacks organizers, past and present. +1s welcome.

What about Covid?

Everyone who comes must be fully vaccinated.

When is it?

Saturday, May 21st — doors open at 6pm; please arrive before 7pm.

Where is this?

The LUMINA: 201 Folsom St, San Francisco, CA 94105

How do I get there from Stanford?

We'll have a bus from Stanford leaving at 5:15pm and returning... later. Let us know in the RSVP if you have to be back by a particular time. RSVP by Friday, May 6th to make sure you get a seat on the bus.

How do I get there otherwise?

It’s an 8 minute walk from the Embarcadero BART station. If you’re driving, free valet parking is available in the building. Enter the Lumina garage from Beale St, between Folsom and Harrison. At the party you’ll be able to collect a valet sticker, which you can use for your car at the end of the night.

Will there be dinner?

Yes. Please note any dietary restrictions on the RSVP.

Can I bring a guest?

Yes! Please indicate on the RSVP form.
