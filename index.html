FAQ

Who is coming?

Where is this?

Doors open at 8PM. Please arrive before 9PM.

How much does this cost?

Dress code: Suits and dresses only. For accessories, think ties, red nail polish, red lipstick, and gold jewelry. We will have some accessories available, such as fedoras, gun holsters, and fake guns, but you're welcome to bring your own. Some pictures for reference here, here, and here.

Should I bring other clothes?

You might want to bring a bathing suit. There is a jacuzzi with a great view.

What should I expect?

We're going for a more intimate, whimsical experience. Expect high-end cocktails, ballroom dancing, a gambling den... and secret blood rituals.

What are the rules?

Everything is "off the record".

Can I bring a guest?

Yes, but please invite no more than one person without asking us first. Whoever you invite, please have them register here at least two days in advance so that we can prepare.
